About

Peter is an experienced software developer and data scientist, specializing in data access, analysis, and visualization. He is passionate about healthcare, especially focused on technology to boost patient-centered care. He earned his Bachelor's degree in Biomedical Engineering, with an emphasis in Biocomputing, in 2018, and his Master's degree in Computational Mathematical and Statistical Sciences, in 2021.

Worldly Experience

I am not currently employed by EWH but I will always carry the memories in my heart.
