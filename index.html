Travel

I am honored to be an alumnus of the Engineering World Health Summer Institutes. Since being a program participant in 2016, I have been contracted by them twice as an On the Ground Coordinator - once in Rwanda and once in Uganda. I am not currently employed by EWH but I will always carry the memories in my heart.

While abroad, I documented my experiences in the form of a travel blog. I have added to it since my trips to share updates and any talks I have given on my experiences.
